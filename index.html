The belly button is one of the habitats closest to us, and yet it remains relatively unexplored.
In January 2011, the Belly Button Biodiversity project was launched in order to investigate the microbes inhabiting our navels and the factors that might influence the microscopic life calling this protected, moist patch of skin home.
